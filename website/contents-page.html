Framework Documentation

ZORBA Framework Contents

The complete ZORBA reference architecture — nine documents that define how enterprises operate in the agentic era. Read in order for the full picture, or jump to what matters most.

01

The ZORBA Manifesto

Why enterprise architecture must be redesigned for the agentic era. The founding vision for a world where humans and AI agents co-exist as a unified workforce — and why the human/agent boundary is the defining architectural question of modern enterprise.

02

Framework Architecture

The six-layer structural hierarchy — from Organisational Unit down to Work Item — with strategy as a cross-cutting alignment overlay. Every layer defines what humans do, what agents do, and what governance applies.

03

The Blended Workforce Model

A deep dive into how humans and AI agents work together. Agent taxonomies, collaboration patterns, trust levels, autonomy frameworks, and the workforce composition decisions that define the agentic enterprise.

04

Domain Reference Model

Twelve enterprise domains — six Value Chain, six Management Functions — each with an agentic maturity profile showing which activities are ripe for agent participation and where the human/agent workforce split is heading.

05

Agentic Enterprise Patterns

Reusable patterns for how AI agents participate in enterprise operations — from Strategy Formulation Assistant to Autonomous Process Monitor. Each pattern defines its applicable layers, human touchpoints, and governance requirements.

06

Governance & Trust Framework

How do you govern a blended workforce? Agent accountability, decision authority matrices, escalation protocols, audit trails, trust calibration, and the principle that human override is always possible but not always necessary.

07

Information Model

The universal business object graph — node types, edge types, composition rules, and traversal patterns. Framework-agnostic by design: supports ZORBA, APQC/PCF, or any custom taxonomy on the same underlying meta-model.

08

Industry Editions

ZORBA Core is universal. Industry Editions tailor the framework for specific verticals — technology service providers, healthcare, financial services, manufacturing, and more. Same architecture, configured for your industry's reality.

09

Glossary

Key terms and definitions — both traditional enterprise architecture concepts and the new agentic vocabulary that ZORBA introduces. The shared language for humans, architects, technologists, and AI agents.
