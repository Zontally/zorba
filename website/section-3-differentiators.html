Why ZORBA

Not Another Process Framework
with AI Bolted On

Agents Are First-Class Citizens

ZORBA doesn't treat AI as a technology layer or an automation add-on. Agents are workforce participants with defined roles, autonomy levels, trust profiles, and governance requirements — woven into every layer of the architecture.

Built for Complex Organisations

Organisational Units nest recursively — holding companies, subsidiaries, business units, brands, divisions, regions. Each unit can adopt a different industry edition. No forced symmetry. No artificial constraints.

Value Chain + Management Functions

Twelve reference domains classified as either Value Chain (industry-specific, differentiating) or Management Functions (industry-agnostic, enabling). Customise your value chain heavily. Adopt management functions closer to the reference.

Industry Editions

ZORBA Core is universal. Industry Editions pre-configure the framework for specific verticals — technology service providers, healthcare, financial services, manufacturing, and more. Same underlying model, tailored for your reality.

Machine-Readable by Design

The architecture isn't a PDF for a shelf. It's a queryable information model that agents use at runtime to understand their context, authority, and boundaries. Architecture becomes the operating system of the enterprise.

Framework-Agnostic

Already using APQC? ITIL? A custom taxonomy? ZORBA's information model supports any classification scheme. Adopt ZORBA's 12 domains, map your existing framework, or run them side by side.
