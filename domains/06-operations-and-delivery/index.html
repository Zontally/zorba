    Domain 6: Operations & Delivery (471829)¶

    Executing with precision and adaptability

    Classification: Value Chain Version: v0.1 (Draft)

    Overview¶

    This domain encompasses the enterprise's ability to plan, execute, and continuously improve its core operational activities — whether producing physical goods, delivering services, or running hybrid fulfilment models. It covers the full span from operational planning through delivery execution, quality assurance, and ongoing operational refinement. In a ZORBA-enabled enterprise, operations is where agents move from advisory to executive roles — scheduling production runs, monitoring quality in real time, detecting anomalies before they escalate, and orchestrating exception handling across the delivery chain.
