# Domain 4: Sales (358741)

*Converting relationships into revenue*

**Classification:** Value Chain
**Version:** v0.1 (Draft)

---

## Overview

This domain encompasses the enterprise's ability to identify, pursue, win, and grow customer relationships that generate revenue. It spans the full sales spectrum from strategic territory design and pipeline management through to deal execution, account development, and revenue operations.

In a ZORBA-enabled enterprise, sales is where **agents handle the data-heavy mechanics of selling** — scoring leads, maintaining CRM hygiene, generating proposals, and forecasting revenue — while humans focus on relationship building, complex negotiation, and strategic account development.
