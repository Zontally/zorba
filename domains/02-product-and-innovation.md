# Domain 2: Product & Innovation (482917)

*Creating what the market needs next*

**Classification:** Value Chain
**Version:** v0.1 (Draft)

---

## Overview

This domain encompasses the enterprise's ability to envision, design, build, and evolve the products and services that deliver value to its customers and markets. It spans from early-stage ideation and research through to product lifecycle management and retirement, including the intellectual property that protects competitive advantage.

In a ZORBA-enabled enterprise, this domain is where **agentic capabilities most visibly augment human creativity** — with agents accelerating research synthesis, automating testing cycles, generating design variations, and providing real-time product analytics that inform every decision from concept through sunset.
