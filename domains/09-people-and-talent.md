# Domain 9: People & Talent (482731)

*Attracting and orchestrating the blended workforce*

**Classification:** Management Function
**Version:** v0.1 (Draft)

---

## Overview

This domain encompasses the enterprise's ability to attract, develop, engage, and retain the human talent it needs — while simultaneously provisioning, calibrating, and managing the agent workforce that operates alongside them. It spans the full employee lifecycle from workforce planning through separation, and extends into the uniquely ZORBA territory of blended workforce orchestration.

In a ZORBA-enabled enterprise, this domain is where **the human and agent workforces are designed, composed, and harmonised** — ensuring that every role, whether filled by a person or an agent, is deliberately planned, appropriately skilled, and continuously optimised.
