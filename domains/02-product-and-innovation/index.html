    Domain 2: Product/Service & Innovation (482917)¶

    Creating what customers and markets need next

    Classification: Value Chain Version: v0.1 (Draft)

    Overview¶

    This domain encompasses the enterprise's ability to envision, design, build, and evolve the products and services that deliver value to its customers and markets. It spans from early-stage ideation and research through to lifecycle management and retirement, early-stage ideation and research through to lifecycle management and retirement, including the intellectual property that protects competitive advantage. Whether an organisation delivers physical products, software, professional services, or a combination, this domain covers how offerings are conceived, validated, brought to market, and sustained. The specific shape varies by industry — product-led firms emphasise engineering and release cycles; service-led firms emphasise methodology design and offering packaging.
