# Domain 1: Strategy & Governance (738201)

*Setting direction and ensuring accountability*

**Classification:** Management Function
**Version:** v0.1 (Draft)

---

## Overview

This domain encompasses the enterprise's ability to define its purpose, set strategic direction, govern its operations, manage enterprise-wide performance, and ensure the organisation adapts and transforms to remain competitive. It is the domain where long-term intent is formulated, corporate accountability is established, and enterprise-wide coordination is orchestrated.

In a ZORBA-enabled enterprise, this domain also governs **how the blended workforce itself is designed and overseen** — ensuring that the human/agent operating model is a deliberate strategic choice, not an emergent accident.
