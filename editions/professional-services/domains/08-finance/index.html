    Domain 8: Finance (593417)¶

    Industry Edition: ZORBA for Professional Services

    This is the ZORBA for Professional Services variant of this domain.

    Funding growth and protecting the bottom line

    Classification: Management Function Version: v0.1 (Draft)

    Overview¶

    This domain encompasses the enterprise's ability to plan, allocate, record, and report on financial resources — ensuring fiscal discipline, regulatory compliance, and the financial intelligence needed to support strategic decision-making. It spans from long-range financial planning through day-to-day transaction processing to external reporting and audit readiness. In a ZORBA-enabled enterprise, finance is a domain of high automation potential in transactional processes (accounts payable, reconciliation, reporting) while retaining strong human leadership in judgement-intensive areas such as treasury strategy, tax planning, and stakeholder communication.
