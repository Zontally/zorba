    Domain 12: Corporate Services (751284)¶

    Industry Edition: ZORBA for Enterprise Software

    This is the ZORBA for Enterprise Software variant of this domain.

    Running the engine that keeps everything moving

    Classification: Management Function Version: v0.1 (Draft)

    Overview¶

    This domain encompasses the enterprise's supporting infrastructure — the facilities, communications, administrative services, and shared capabilities that enable every other domain to function effectively. It is the operational backbone that maintains the physical and organisational environment, communicates the enterprise story, manages sustainability obligations, and delivers efficient shared services. In a ZORBA-enabled enterprise, corporate services increasingly leverage agents for high-volume administrative workflows, facilities monitoring, and communications distribution — freeing human professionals to focus on strategic programmes and stakeholder relationships.
